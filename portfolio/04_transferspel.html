Transferspel (De Telegraaf)

Complete technical and graphical realisation for this online, multiuser concept created for Factor5 (for "De Telegraaf " Netherlands).

Screenshots

Involved marketing parties ..

  • Voetbal International
  • Canal+
  • EA Sports
  • Perry Sports
  • Tickets.nl
  • De Grote Voetbal Encyclopedie

Detail:

Users can be a soccermanager for a complete football season. They compete to attain a top-10 ranking by playing wisely in the following ways ..

  • buying and selling players (trading),
  • answering questions in the field of soccer,
  • challenging other online-users for a 1-on-1 challenge.

Features:

Transferspel features the following ..

  • 100% Flash interface (see screenshots)
    which enables unique design and interaction methodoligies unseen in traditional HTML interfaces
  • Online Messaging
  • Online Trading
  • Has unique client synchronisation mechanism. If anything in a userstate on the server changes this is automaticly pushed to the client.

Results:

Transferspel delivered ..

  • 3000 contenders.
  • 15432.80 one-on-one quizes a month (contenders playing eachother)
  • 15211 trades a month (contenders transferring players)
