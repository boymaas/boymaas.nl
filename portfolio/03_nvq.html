Nationale Voetbal Quiz (Voetbal International)

Complete technical and graphical realisation for this online, multiuser concept created for Factor5 (for "Voetbal International" Netherlands).

Detail:

People can be a soccermanager for a footballseason. They compete to attain a top 10 ranking by playing wisely in the following ways ..

  • answering questions in the field of soccer,
  • challenging other online-users for a 1-on-1 challenge.

Features:

The "Nationale Voetbal Quiz" features the following ..

  • 100% Flash interface (see screenshots)
  • Online Messaging
  • Online Trading
  • Has unique client synchronisation mechanism. If anything in a userstate on the server changes this is automaticly pushed to the client.

Results:

The "Nationale Voetbal Quiz" delivered ..

  • over 20,000 subscriptions
  • periods of 250-350 concurrent users online doing messaging and online quizes. (running on a single webserver!!!)
  • 350,000+ one-on-one quizes in first 3 months time. (100.000+ a month) !!
  • 750,000+ emails spread in first 3 months to 20,000+ userbase!!
