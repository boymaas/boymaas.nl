  • Freelance projects

    Some highlights of my freelance work

Introduction

I own a small Dutch development studio located in Den Haag, founded in March 1998 with the continuing goal of creating innovative, exciting network-based concepts. Since 1999, we have completed projects for almost a dozen clients such as popular brands like Factor5, Voetbal International, Coca-Cola, Sponsorloterij; media groups like Dutch newspaper venture De Telegraaf ; and telecommunications operator Telfort BV (British Telecom).

Method

We work as a small, independent development shop with a relatively low overhead and receiving new projects based on our reputation for innovation and creativity and completing projects on-time and within budget. We specialise in mixed media, cross-platform inter-working, out-of-the-box thinking and designing experiences with extremely high replay factors. Internet, mobile, network games, promotions, advertising, online gambling and youth markets.

Services

If you have a new interactive challenge, let us help you take it to the next level. With over ten years of online system development - Tagged can work with you on new projects from concept to final design. We prefer new challenges and are most excited with projects that generate high brand value and direct revenue based on participation. We have an extensive and specialised network to help design applications targeting different customer segments, and work from concepts and customisation - to programming and project management.
